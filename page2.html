Step by Step guides for video games

The games on this website

Outlast

Outlast is a Horror game that takes place at a metal institution. You are a reporter who must investigate the company that owns the mental facility and expose the wrong doings that the company has done to the patients.

Sekiro Shadows Die Twice

Sekiro Shadows Die Twice is an action/adventure game where you play as a unknown shinobi. You must face various bosses that get harder each time you face another one. This game is a part of the souls genre which is a genre of games with a long story and a lot of very hard bosses with some bosses from these games being considered the hardest bosses in gaming history.

Resident Evil 3

Resident Evil 3 is an action/horror game. This game is a part of a huge series of games being the resident evil games. In this game you play as a police officer that is being hunted down by a monster named nemesis and you have to deal with a zombie outbreak as well. This is a traditional action/horror game meaning that you play the story and thats it. This game also connects with all of the other resident evil games which can help introduce people to the series.
